About

I'm a microeconomic theorist who does work at the intersection of economics and computer science.

Next year, I will be a Saieh Family Fellow at the University of Chicago's Becker-Friedman Institute.

After that, I will be an Assistant Professor of Economics at the Stanford Graduate School of Business.

My recent and upcoming talks include:

  • University of British Columbia (April 2022)
  • Review of Economic Studies Tour (May 2022 in Manchester, Vienna, Paris)
  • University of Bonn (May 2022)
  • ACM Conference on Economics and Computation (July 2022 in Boulder, Colorado)
  • Penn State University (Fall 2022)
